What is Cyber Safety?

Cyber safety means protecting yourself, your personal information, and your digital devices from online threats. It covers simple habits and digital skills that keep you safe while browsing, chatting, shopping, and studying online.

Common Online Risks

  • Phishing: Fake messages or emails that try to trick you into giving personal info.
  • Scams: Fraudulent offers (e.g., fake prizes, job offers, online shopping scams).
  • Malware: Malicious software that can steal or damage data.
  • Identity Theft: When someone uses your personal details to commit fraud.

Why Filipinos Are Vulnerable

High social media use, online shopping, and limited cyber awareness make many people targets. Simple habits like weak passwords or clicking unknown links increase risk.
